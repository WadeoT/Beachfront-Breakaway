About Us

Welcome to Beachfront Breakaway — a luxurious, islander-style retreat for nature-loving, adventurous people who value sustainability and comfort. Each of our beachside homes is fully off-grid, powered by solar energy, and built with recycled materials and natural finishes.

Our goal is to balance the triple bottom line: people, planet, and profit. We offer a unique experience where guests can relax in modern luxury while minimizing their carbon footprint. From rainwater harvesting and composting to net-zero waste systems, every stay supports environmental responsibility.

Our mission is to provide older couples and families a peaceful escape into nature while enjoying five-star amenities. And our vision is to expand into 5–10 beautiful, remote locations — empowering guests to connect with nature and learn sustainable living.
