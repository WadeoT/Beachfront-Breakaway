Our Mission

We created Beachfront Breakaway to give people a way to reconnect with nature — without leaving a mark on it. Located on the sands of Mission Beach, our tiny home is 100% off-grid, beautifully designed, and totally immersed in the natural world.

Why We’re Different

  • Solar-powered with no grid connection
  • Filtered rainwater and composting toilet
  • Locally sourced furnishings and materials
