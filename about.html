Our Story

Beachfront Breakaway is a luxurious island retreat for nature-loving adventurers. Completely off-grid and environmentally sustainable, our tiny homes offer modern beachy elegance with zero carbon footprint.

We aim to pay off construction within two years and land within five by offering seasonal high-end stays. Our homes use recycled materials, solar power, and rainwater systems to operate without harming the local ecosystem.
