Welcome to Your Luxury Beach Retreat

At Beachfront Breakaway, we offer the perfect balance between modern luxury and natural serenity. Located in remote coastal areas, our tiny homes are ideal for couples and families seeking a peaceful escape powered by nature.

About Us

Beachfront Breakaway is a luxurious, eco-friendly retreat designed for nature-loving, adventurous people who seek comfort and sustainability. Our tiny homes are completely off-grid, powered by solar energy, and built with recycled and natural materials to ensure a net-zero carbon footprint.

We balance the triple bottom line of people, planet, and profit, providing unique experiences while protecting the environment and supporting the local community.

Our mission is to offer affordable luxury beachside stays for older couples and families wanting a peaceful escape, while educating guests on reducing their carbon footprint.

We envision expanding to build multiple homes in isolated, pristine locations, promoting eco-tourism and sustainability education.

Features

  • Completely off-grid tiny homes with solar power
  • Water reclaiming and rainwater storage systems
  • Zero waste policy with composting and recycling
  • Built with natural and recycled materials
  • Luxurious, modern interiors with beachy, natural aesthetics
  • Ideal for families and couples looking for unique beach escapes

Location

Our homes are nestled in remote, pristine coastal locations where guests can fully immerse in nature. Enjoy secluded beaches, spectacular sunrises, and peaceful surroundings far from city noise.

Each location supports local ecosystems and communities, ensuring our footprint remains light and beneficial.
