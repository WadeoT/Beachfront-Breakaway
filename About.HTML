About Us

At Beachfront Breakaway, we believe in the power of nature to refresh the soul. Our mission is to offer a high-end, environmentally friendly experience for families and couples seeking a peaceful escape from everyday life. Nestled in secluded coastal areas, our tiny homes are designed to be completely off-grid, using solar energy, rainwater harvesting, and sustainable building practices to reduce our impact on the planet.

We balance the triple bottom line — People, Planet, and Profit — by ensuring our retreats are luxurious yet affordable, sustainable yet comfortable, and exclusive yet welcoming. Our vision is to expand to 5–10 homes in isolated destinations where guests can enjoy nature, learn about sustainable living, and truly unwind.

Whether you're watching the sunset from your private deck or waking up to the sound of the ocean, we aim to deliver a truly unforgettable stay that leaves you refreshed and inspired.
