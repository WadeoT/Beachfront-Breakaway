About Us

Luxury. Sustainability. Nature — All in One.

Who We Are

Beachfront Breakaway is a modern, luxurious short-stay retreat designed for those who value nature, comfort, and sustainability. Our eco-conscious tiny homes are situated in stunning, remote beachfront locations — offering a high-end escape without harming the environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an affordable but luxurious coastal retreat for older couples and families looking to escape the stress of everyday life. We believe in the healing power of nature and have designed our homes to be completely off-grid, powered by solar energy, and zero-waste. Visitors not only enjoy a premium getaway, but also learn how to reduce their carbon footprint.

Our Vision

We aim to build 5–10 sustainable homes in remote locations, creating a network of unique travel destinations where relaxation meets education. Each stay is a step toward a greener future, empowering guests with tools and inspiration to live more sustainably.

Why Sustainability Matters

We operate by the Triple Bottom Line: People, Planet, and Profit. By balancing customer satisfaction, environmental stewardship, and responsible profitability, we ensure Beachfront Breakaway thrives long-term — without compromising ethics or quality. Our goal is to pay off construction within two years and land within five, all while staying carbon-neutral.
