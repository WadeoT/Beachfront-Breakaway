About Us

Beachfront Breakaway is a luxurious islander retreat designed for nature-loving adventurers with a taste for modern comfort. Our eco-friendly, off-grid tiny homes are nestled in pristine, isolated locations along the coast, giving you a private paradise that's both luxurious and sustainable.

We care deeply about the triple bottom line: People, Planet, and Profit. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable getaway while being net-zero, self-sustaining, and supportive of the local ecosystem. With solar power, rainwater collection, and zero waste systems, your stay helps preserve the beauty you came to experience.
