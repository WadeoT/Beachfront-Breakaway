About Us

Beachfront Breakaway is a luxurious yet eco-friendly retreat designed for those seeking peace, nature, and comfort. Built on the principles of sustainability, our homes are completely off-grid, powered by the sun, and leave zero waste behind. Every detail — from recycled cement walls to reclaimed rainwater systems — has been thoughtfully chosen to protect and enhance our beautiful beachside environment.

Our mission is simple: to offer older couples and families a chance to escape the chaos of everyday life and reconnect with each other and the planet. When you stay with us, you're not only indulging in modern comfort and secluded natural beauty, you're also part of a movement toward a more sustainable future.
