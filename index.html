About Us

Welcome to Beachfront Breakaway, your perfect short stay accommodation by the sea. We offer a serene and eco-friendly escape where you can relax, recharge, and enjoy the natural beauty of the coastline without sacrificing modern comforts.

Features and Locations

Each of our beachfront tiny homes offers:

  • Private deck with ocean views
  • Eco-friendly design and solar power
  • Luxury bedding and rain showers
  • Free Wi-Fi and smart home features
  • Walking distance to cafes and surf beaches

Locations currently available:

  • Noosa Heads
  • Caloundra
  • Bribie Island
  • Byron Bay
